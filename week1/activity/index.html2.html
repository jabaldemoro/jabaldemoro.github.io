Scrambled Eggs
Eggs is one of my favourite foods. Here is a deliciously rich scrambled

Ingredients

Method
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
  1. Melt Butter
  2. Mix egg and cream in bowl
  3. Once Butter is melted add in egg
  4. fold eggs using spatula
  5. When eggs is still moist remove the heat(it will continue to cook onto the plate until served)
